What You'll Learn

Program Curriculum

A comprehensive, industry-aligned curriculum designed by experienced Business Analysts.

01 BA Fundamentals & SDLC
Introduction to Business Analysis, SDLC models (Waterfall, Agile, Hybrid), roles and responsibilities of a BA, stakeholder identification and management.
02 Requirement Gathering Techniques
Interviews, workshops, surveys, brainstorming, observation techniques. Requirements elicitation, analysis, validation and prioritization (MoSCoW).
03 BRD / FRD Documentation
Writing Business Requirements Documents, Functional Requirements Documents, use cases, user stories, acceptance criteria and traceability matrix.
04 UML Diagrams & Process Modeling
Activity diagrams, use case diagrams, sequence diagrams, state machine diagrams, class diagrams. BPMN process modeling with Draw.io and Visio.
05 Agile & Scrum Methodology
Scrum framework, sprint planning, daily standups, retrospectives, backlog management with Jira. Writing user stories and epics.
06 SQL for Business Analysts
SQL fundamentals, SELECT, JOINs, GROUP BY, subqueries, data analysis queries. Working with real databases for BA reporting needs.
07 Power BI & Data Visualization
Power BI Desktop, data import, DAX formulas, interactive dashboards, report publishing. Tableau basics for visual analytics.
08 Real-Time Projects & Capstones
End-to-end BA documentation for Banking, Healthcare and E-commerce projects. BRD, FRD, wireframes, user stories, sprint boards and final presentations.
